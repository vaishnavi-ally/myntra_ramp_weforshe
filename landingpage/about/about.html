About Us

Welcome to Myntra Ramp !!!. Fashion enthusiasts, we invite you to showcase your style expertise and compete for exciting prizes from Myntra. You'll be tasked with creating a comprehensive outfit, including top and bottom wear, footwear, jewelry, and accessories. The most impressive ensemble, showcasing exceptional flair, will be awarded top honors. Your design must align with the theme 'Monsoon Radiance,' capturing the essence of the season with elegance and poise. ...
To participate in this extraordinary theme-based competition, simply register on the Myntra app and submit the requisite form, providing details about yourself and the outfit you've meticulously crafted. The rewards will be bestowed based on rankings determined by public votes. You can also track your progress on the leaderboard. Please note that all outfits must be created exclusively using garments from Myntra's own extensive catalogue. We wish all fashion aficionados the very best of luck in this exciting endeavor!
